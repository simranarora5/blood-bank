BLOOD DONATION SERVICES

Our main aim is to make the process of blood donation a pleasant experience for the donors. We stress on safe, good quality and
rational practices in the process of blood donation. Proper pre donation counselling and relevant medical / physical examination by
a medical officer are carried out both on voluntary donors as well as the patient relatives who come to donate blood.
Plateletapheresis is performed regularly with the help of automated cell separators in order to meet the demands for platelet transfusions.
